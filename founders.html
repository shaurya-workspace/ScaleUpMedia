Meet the Founder

Shaurya Goel

Founder & CEO, ScaleUp Media

Shaurya Goel is the Founder and CEO of ScaleUp Media, a digital marketing agency focused on helping brands grow and stand out in the digital world. With a strong interest in branding, content creation, and performance marketing, Shaurya started ScaleUp Media with a vision to deliver creative, result-driven solutions for startups, creators, and businesses.


He believes that marketing is not just about promotion, but about building trust and long-term relationships with audiences. By combining strategy, creativity, and modern digital tools, Shaurya aims to help clients scale faster and smarter in today’s competitive online landscape.
